Welcome to Steam Capsulator

Generate every Steam store & library capsule image from one background and an optional logo — crop, refine, and export in seconds.

1
UploadAdd a background image — you'll confirm or tweak the capsule sizes, then generate. Optionally add a logo, or skip it if your art already includes one. Resize any capsule later with the small resize button beside it in the sidebar.
2
Select & positionPick a capsule on the left, then click the logo or background on the canvas to select it and drag / scroll (or pinch on touch) to position — or use the X/Y/scale fields.
3
Style itAdd background effects (blur, tint, vignette, shade) and logo shadow / outline / glow for legibility. Need a different shot for one size? Override that capsule’s image.
4
Preview allOpen “Preview all” to see every capsule side by side and jump straight to any one.
5
ExportDownload a single image or all as a ZIP, with an optional filename prefix. Names match each Steam size.
